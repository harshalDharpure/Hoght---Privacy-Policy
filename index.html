Privacy Policy (hoght)

Effective date: March 31, 2026
App name: hoght
Contact: hsock27@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how hoght (“we”, “our”, “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

1) Information we collect

Account information

When you sign up or log in, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (handled by Firebase Authentication; we do not store your password ourselves)

Content you create

When you use the app, we collect and store:

  • Thoughts you post
  • Comments you post
  • Reaction counts (“I feel this” count)

Technical information

Firebase may collect certain device/app information for security and functionality (for example: device identifiers and IP address), according to Firebase’s services.

2) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your thoughts and comments in the app
  • Sync content across sessions and devices
  • Maintain app security and prevent abuse

3) Where your data is stored

hoght uses Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication for login
  • Cloud Firestore to store user profiles, thoughts, and comments

Your data is stored on Firebase servers and processed according to Firebase and Google’s terms.

4) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only:

  • With Firebase/Google as our infrastructure provider to operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

5) Data retention

We keep your account and content for as long as your account remains active or as needed to operate the app.

If you delete a thought from your profile, it is removed from our Firestore database. (Some related data such as comments may remain unless explicitly deleted.)

6) Your choices and controls

You can:

  • Log out at any time
  • Delete your own thoughts from your profile
  • Contact us to request deletion of your account data (see Contact section)

7) Security

We use Firebase security features and Firestore security rules to help protect your data. However, no online system is 100% secure.

8) Children’s privacy

hoght is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9) Third-party services

hoght uses:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore)

You can review Google/Firebase privacy information here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” when changes are made.
